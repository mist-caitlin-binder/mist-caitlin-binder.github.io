Organic Chemistry in Nature

Biomolecules are organic chemicals made by living organisms. In fact, you are made of biomolecules - including your hair, skin, and nails!

Carvone is an organic molecule synthesized by many plants, including spearmint and cannabis.

Let's look at how carvone is drawn in full and abbreviated forms.




Abbreviated AKA "Zig Zig" Organic Structures
Most carbon (C) and hydrogen (H) atoms are not explictly shown!
  • Carbon is not drawn is implied at end of a line or where 2+ lines meet. Carbon makes 4 bonds (drawn as lines).
  • Hydrogens: any of carbon's 4 bonds that are not already shown implies that the remaining bonds are to hydrogen. If only 1 bond (line) to carbon is shown, then the remaining 3 bonds are to hydrogens.
  • Exception: Hydrogens are drawn when bonded to any atom besides carbon, including OH groups found in alcohols.
  • Oxygen (O) and all other atoms are drawn with their atomic symbol.
