Learning Objectives
  1. Given a job aid, you will be able to list the five steps to make cannabis tincture
  2. Given the necessary ingredients and job aid, you will be able to make cannabis tincture from food-grade liquid solvents (high-proof alcohol or vegetable oil). The standard is for the tincture to achieve the your desired psychoactive effects of marijuana by ingestion, as a substitute for harmful smoking
  3. You will be able to describe the purpose of grinding and decarboxylating cannabis flower before adding solvent
  4. Given corresponding diagrams, you will be able to explain the basic chemistry of the extraction and filtration steps to make cannabis tincture; specifically how the THC and solvent molecules interact with each other
  5. Given pairs of chemical structures, you will be able to select which is an atom vs. a molecule
  6. Given the abbreviated "zig zag" structure of an organic molecule, you will be able to determine its chemical formula (number of carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen atoms in the molecule)
  7. Given an informative website/job aid, you will be to list the state and federal laws on possession and use of marijuana in your local area
  8. Given the Last Prisoner Project's "Letter Writing Guide," you will be able to list the steps for writing a letter to an incarcerated individual.
